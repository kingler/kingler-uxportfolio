Hi there my name is Kingler Bercy. I am a user experience designer with extensive background in designing intuitive user friendly applications for the web, desktop screens, and mobile devices. My design approach is based on four core universal principles of design: Minimalism(less is more), Information aesthetics (simplying complexity thru imagery), Heuristic evaluation (meaningful data), User Centered Design (descovering the needs, nants, and desires of the user). I've worked with both small business and multinational fortune five hundred companies on designing user interfaces for complex financial management systems.
